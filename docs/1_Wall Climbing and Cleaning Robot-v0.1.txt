Project Name: Wall Climbing and Cleaning Robot (for Cobwebs) - v1

Team: Joseph Bolujo and David Arinde

Aim: To design and implement a robot capable of climbing walls and 		 cleaning then (specifically cleaning cobwebs on the walls). 
Note: the aim is still too generic (a performance target, environment of use and constraint should be defined eventually).

Objectives: 
- To design a robot capable of (i) climbing walls and (ii) cleaning walls
- To simulate a robot capable of i and ii above
- To implement a robot capable of i and ii above
- To evaluate the robot based on assigned metrics,

Target Users: Places where cobwebs exist and need to be cleaned, whether they are reachable by traditional means or not. Our target audience exists in every industry.
Note: Target audience too wide at the moment. Let's start small and scale from there
