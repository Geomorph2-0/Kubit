# Project Name: Wall-Climbing and Cleaning Robot (for Cobwebs) - v0.1

Team: Joseph Bolujo and David Arinde

## Aim:

To design and implement a wall-climbing robot capable of climbing painted vertical concrete walls and removing cobwebs with an accuracy >= 80%.

## Objectives:

- To design a robot capable of (i) climbing walls and (ii) cleaning walls
- To simulate a robot capable of i and ii above
- To implement a robot capable of i and ii above
- To evaluate the robot based on assigned metrics,

## Environment Consideration:

Wall Material: Painted Concrete
Surface Roughness: ?
Surface Inclination: Vertical

## Target Users: 

Places where cobwebs exist and need to be cleaned, whether they are reachable by traditional means or not. Our target audience exists in every industry.
